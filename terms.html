description TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Medlife Foundation is grateful to all of its supporters including those who contribute time, goods and/or money to help us continue doing our work.
Medlife Foundation maintains strict Donor Privacy Policies in order to protect the Integrity and Privacy of Personal Information gathered from Our Supporters and Visitors through all of our Channels, Social Networking Sites as well as to our Websites and Mobile App and those people who donate through them.
Medlife Foundation does not Sell your Personal Information to any organization or individual. We will not indulge in any of these activities related to the individual's personal information to any other organization or individual other than that necessary to transact, fulfill and or account for online donations that you have authorized.
Medlife Foundation will only use your Phone Number and E-mail address if we need to Verify Information or to Appreciate for your Contribution, Ask for Suggestions, Request Assistance, or Send Invitation for an Upcoming Event.

What information do we collect?

When you make a donation, attend an event or volunteer with Medlife, we may ask for information such as your name, address, email address, phone number and other relevant information.
Medlife Foundation uses cookies to facilitate authentication and personalization across our web systems. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your Web browser from a Web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Medlife Foundation uses only anonymous information (such as IP address) to track how our online visitors use our website. This data is sometimes stored using cookies.

How do we use your information?

Medlife Foundation uses personally identifiable information you provide about yourself:
  • To process your donation.
  • To send you a receipt for your donation.
  • To send you a Note of Appreciation for the Contribution or Volunteer ship.
  • To respond to the Queries related to Medlife.
  • To send Additional Information related to Upcoming Medlife Projects.
  • To send Invitations for Upcoming Events that may interest you.

Medlife Foundation uses non-identifying basic information (aggregate data) to analyze site use and to improve the content and design of the site. Medlife Foundation may share aggregate data such as how many people attended an event, how many volunteers currently working and worked, Average Donation Amount etc. contributed in a year by Sponsors and Partners. These statistics will not include any Personal Information. Medlife Foundation will not use Personal Information in any way other than as described under Terms & Conditions. Exceptions to these are limited to sharing personal information to cooperate with law enforcement officials.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of the e-mail updates by clicking the unsubscribe button at the bottom of each email or contacting us directly.

Credit / Debit Card Security

Medlife Foundation is grateful for the donations that help us continue our valuable work. When you donate to Medlife Foundation online, your card information is 100% secured; your credit card number is used only for that particular transaction and is not stored. If you allow us to store your Credit Card or Bank information for future use such as Recurring Donation, such information will be stored by the Payment Services Financial Institution. Medlife Foundation only uses Financial Institution that adheres to the Industry Standards for storing such information.

Is donation secure?

Guarantee. The Security and Confidentiality of your information is our Highest Priority. We use industry-standard SSL (Secure Socket Layer) Technology to protect your information and provide a safe and secure environment for online donations.

Is donation tax-deductible?

Yes, it is. All cash gifts to Medlife Foundation are Tax Deductible under 80G. Medlife Foundation is also FCRA certified to receive foreign currency funds. To claim tax exemption, please keep the donation receipt as your official record.
A donation receipt will be sent to you at the email address that you have provided while making donations, as soon as your donation is processed.

No liability

Our Website has links to other websites that may collect identifiable information about you. Medlife Foundation cannot ensure the privacy practices of other sites and are not responsible for any damages due to them.
