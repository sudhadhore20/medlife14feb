MORE ABOUT US

We have continued our efforts from the year 2016 with tremendous hope and anticipation about Rural and Slum development, Women Empowerment, providing Young Unemployed people with Skill Training and Child Upliftment.

Medlife Foundation strives to work towards finding Effective Healthcare Solutions to the problems faced by people who are not privileged by spreading awareness on Sustainable Menstruation and Development of Children belonging to a weaker section of society. It has been actively working on solving issues related to the Health of People by conducting various healthcare programs & by Innovation of Sustainable Products.

OUR JOURNEY

Few years ago, The founders Bhushan & Sandeep came together for a good reason - to dedicate their time, knowledge, resources and skills in reaching out to those people who need Affordable healthcare services, Medical facilities, Medicine, Menstrual Education, Women and Child development, Women Empowerment, Social entrepreneurship in rural as well as urban areas with many types of social activities and the families in need, irrespective of their caste, creed and status with our only motto — One Help can Change Many Lives.

We believe in providing all the resources to the needy people to support & empower them to make their own destiny and create better impact to bring a smile on every face in our country.Medlife Foundation is helping in solving menstrual problem for the needy menstruating girls and women from rural areas and other vulnerable communities to move on in life with the passion of providing Sustainable Healthcare facilities, Sustainable Menstruation, Menstrual Education, Women Entrepreneurship and Environment in Various Aspects.

We have always held a belief that through Innovation and Efforts.

The Youth can transform our country with their new ideas and skills. Whether rich or poor, the next generation has the power to change this world for the better.The broad spectrum of our activities and their success rate speak about dedicated work done by Medlife Foundation.
