Mental health for woman

Generally women’s mental health tends to suffer because of low self-esteem, low self-worth, and lack of Confidence that develop during their teen years and slowly develop into their adulthood. Until the age of 12 , there is no much difference in the mental health of boys and girls. As getting older, Women develop high pressure on themselves as their community becomes larger or they enter into workspace. Trying to balance their multiple role as a wife and a mom and an employee and a daughterin-law . In some cases it is making them stronger but in some it becomes so vulnerable. Balancing these along with the mental stress and depression becomes a great uphill for women to climb. If one lacks skills in work-life balance they become prone to chronic illness. The scenario of present cases are totally different. Right from the hide and seek game in childhood till they hide completely underground after death, unfortunately society has given the bitter experience of ‘living in fear' . There is deep carving in every women’s mind and soul that ‘safer place is nowhere’. This is actually the biggest cause of the mental breakdown and mental health issues common in women.

 

Wearing shorts and shirt, chocolate in mouth, not much words known to speak, wearing a big smile in face, a kid, played in the street, in front of her house. While everyone enjoying her innocence and tiny little feet playing and running around, there came hands, with no heart to rape her. There are many Asifa Bano, playing freely around without knowing the cruel society where she lives in.It is estimated that around 27.8% girlchilds were raped to death in India in 2021 and without surprise it is still in increasing rate. In such a case, how will every girl born will feel safe and have a great mental health. Think of all the moms of those little girlchilds.



How will a mother or father will allow their female child to play around. It ahs been really worst in India, that Department of External Affairs of Government of India has made an announcement that, this nation is not safe for women from other countries. A cute little rabbit in Lion’s den, knowing which place she lives in, how can she have a better mental health. Well, the lion can’t the see little hole, which the rabbit saw. Yes, in this dangerous society for women, there are living example like Rewati Ranjan, Natasha Joel and the list continues.These women gave us the tips to find the way out of the unsafeness and the mental boundaries. They undergone many tribulations right from their childhood. They were raped and used, yet they survived to shout out the voice of women on the same table.

 


 

They gave us the paths to lead a peaceful life , amidst the noises of the world. According to them, don’t make your mind believe that you are a curse, that you are a burden, don’t get sick and tired of being numb in the past. It takes a minute to take up a step in the present. Nobody is gonna hold your back or care about your mental health. It’s you, you as women, you have to make sure to stand firm against the evils of world and not to run away from it. You as a women have faced many pains and struggles, why can’t this also be passing cloud. Make every problem and every circumstance, to pass by, and don’t take into heart. You are born to sit in the thrown, with a crown, family on your lap and workspace on your feet. Be a queen, inspire the world.



To the young women around, below the age of 35,some may face intellectual disabilities, whom the family might be forcing you to get married. Some may undergo physical pains caused by mental stress in workspace. Some women, due to the trauma of the past bad events, may face difficulty in leading a peaceful married life. Mental health is definitely not an urban issue. Women usually won’t shout out the pain they are undergoing. Some may due shyness or fear or some are made or forced to be silent. So it’s important to bring out their mental stress and fears under light.

Causes

Professionals categories the causes of mental issues into three, namely
• Biological
• Social and cultural
• Psychological

 


 

Women’s mental health becomes unique, that it keeps changing according tot their growth cycle. Including the reproductive life stages, maintaining mental health changes as much as the physical. For young girls, puberty becomes the turning point. Before puberty both boys and girls have almost similar disorders like depression and anxiety as they walk through stages of life than men. Many women now ,are well educated and making their own plans and decisions. Yet in some parts of India, women are uprooted from a well known educated environment and put up in a completely unknown place, where she has to survive one again from the beginning, trying to find a path for peaceful life.



She is expected to be adjustable within certain days where others can’t cope up with.She is forced to adjust with a completely new individual whom she held hand with and also with a certain range of unfamiliar people which is not common for men in some parts of India. Culturally some may accept this, but beyond a limit it becomes a stress.Estimations shows that women develop a spike of depression in their early married life. Expectations on women becomes enormous in this phase of life. And many starts to label her as lazy or disobeying or slow girl.

 


 

Tips to reduce mental health issues

• Provide better and safe environment for the child to grow.
• Valuing and validating her and making sure that she has a space to talk freely about the problem she faces.
• Providing her the strong educational background will make her shine.
• Equipping her with conflict resolutions skills and management of emotions.
• Prepare her for motherhood.
• It's all come down to men, to help her with mental health growth. Because men are connected anyhow with women and they also become the part of the risk. If men understands and recognize the women’s vulnerability and support her, she can manage the mental health issues easily. Rather focusing on her weakness, they can strengthen her mental stability.
