World Mental Health day

World Mental Health day is celebrated annually on October 10th globally. It is celebrated to create awareness and education on mental health to public all over the world. It was first celebrated in 1992, initiated by World Federation for Mental Health (WFMH)..

Eating disorder

Feeding and eating disorders affect thousands of people. It usually begins during adolescence. This is the reflection of mental illness like depression. Those people who have doubt about their self, losing self-esteem, loses self care often fall into this disorder. This includes, either the individual reduce the intake of food leading to anorexia nervosa, which is losing weight below a healthy body weight, due to restriction of food or it maybe over intake of food, leading to bulimia nervosa. In this type, people eat large amount of food in a really short period of time.
