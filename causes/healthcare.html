SUSTAINABLE HEALTHCARE

The Sustainable Healthcare System is a system which improves, maintains and restores Health while minimizing Negative Impacts on the Environment and increasing the opportunities to restore and improve it for the well-being of current and future generation.

 
Heart Disease

  • Heart disease is the leading cause of death for people.
  • People may have more subtle symptoms of a heart attack such as upper abdominal pain, lightheadedness or fatigue.
  • All people around the world are facing the threat of heart disease.
  • MEDLIFE FOUNDATION is working towards this direction to raise awareness about heart diseases among people in India by organizing camps to monitor risk factors like high cholesterol, high blood pressure and obesity.



Diabetes

  • Diabetes can lead to serious and costly complications, including heart disease, stroke, amputations, blindness and kidney disease.
  • Women are also likely to develop diabetes during pregnancy.
  • We organize various camps to monitor blood glucose level and other risk factors like hypertension and obesity

 


 
Healthcare

  • It is a disease which is caused by an uncontrolled division of abnormal cells in a part of human body and destroy body tissues.
  • The fact that more number of women have cancer than men in India, reverses the global trend which sees a 25% higher cancer incidence of men than women.
  • Breast cancer, cervical, ovarian and uterine cancers account for 70 percent of cancer cases among women in India.
  • Breast cancer accounts for 27 percent of all cases among women and about 10 percent of them are inherited.
  • BREAST CANCER - A cancer that forms in the cells of breasts (chest region)
  • CERVICAL CANCER - A cancer which occurs in the lowermost part of Uterus or Cervix region.
  • OVARIAN CANCER - A cancer which occurs in the female reproductive organ i.e. Ovaries which produces eggs.
  • UTERINE CANCER - A type of cancer that begins in the Uterus.
  • LEUKAEMIA - A cancer of blood-forming tissues, which prevents the body’s ability to fight infection.
  • PROSTATE CANCER - A cancer in a male’s prostate which produces SEMINAL FLUID What makes it more dangerous for people is mainly the lack of awareness and reluctance to visit the doctor.



Anemia

  • It is a condition in which the blood doesn’t have enough Healthy Red Blood Cells or Lacks no. of Red Blood Cells i.e. Lacks Hemoglobin in the Blood

 

PREVENTIVE HEALTH CHECK- UPS

“Prevention is better than Cure” Preventive Healthcare or Prophylaxis are a series of Medical Tests undertaken to understand the measures needed to prevent a disease. MEDLIFE FOUNDATION has organized several health check-up camps for screening and monitoring of women health including blood pressure monitoring, mammograms, blood glucose level checkup and raising awareness about cervical cancer and that it is mainly caused by an HPV infection and is preventable.
